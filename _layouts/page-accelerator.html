Active Lives Survey - 16/17

We want to get people active using data.

For months, we've been working with activity providers to open up their opportunity data for our OpenActive Accelerator. Now it’s over to you. If you’ve got an innovative idea that uses open opportunity data to help people find and take part in physical activities, we want to hear from you.

Developments in data, social media, VR and AI are having a huge impact on many aspects of people’s lives, removing pain points and creating better experiences for customers.

In the sport and physical activity sector, services designed to get people more active often don’t offer the seamless experiences we are used to in industries like travel or entertainment. Information about what’s available is mostly contained in silos, and little collaboration happens between the different stakeholders in the ecosystem, such as gyms and sport clubs.

That's why we've launched the ODI's OpenActive Accelerator supported by Sport England.
