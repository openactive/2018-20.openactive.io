OpenActive is community-led initiative with an ambition to help people in England get active using open data.

The initiative is stewarded by the Open Data Institute (ODI), supported by Sport England, and formed of organisations and engaged individuals working within the sport and physical activity sector.

The Accelerator Program

OpenActive makes it as easy as possible for people to discover and participate in your activities. It does this by making your activities available everywhere. From websites and apps that attract millions of users, through chieved using the concept of "open data".


Start Ups

Open Data Essentials

Open Data Institute's e-Learning programme developed for the European Commission. This programme has been designed to enable you to discover what open data is and how it is changing the lives of everyone on our planet.

There are 13 lessons for you to explore covering the essentials of open data, how to plan and measure success and how to implement an open data programme technically. This programme is free and should take a maximum of 2-3 hours to complete.
