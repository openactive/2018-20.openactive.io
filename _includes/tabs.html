Where to start?

Activity Providers

#### Activity Providers
![Booking Systems]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/icons/booking-system.svg)
##### Get your activities online You may already use a system to list your activities online. If you don't have one, it's quick and easy to get one. [Get a Booking System]( {{ site.baseurl }}{% link developer.md %}){:class="button-secondary--ghost " }
![Open Data]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/icons/open-data.svg)
##### Tick the box to open your data Find out if your booking system is [OpenActive-enabled] If your booking system isn't listed then you may need to ask them to create an OpenActive feed, or you may need to build your own. [Search instructions for your Booking System]( {{ site.baseurl }}{% link developer.md %}){:class="button-secondary--ghost " }
![Booking Systems]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/icons/data-spec.svg)
##### Your data is now open Your activities are now being published as a "dataset" via a page on your system's website, for anyone to access, use or share. [See open datasets]( {{ site.baseurl }}{% link developer.md %}){:class="button-secondary--ghost " }
![what_open_data.png]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/icons/devices.svg)
##### Your activities are available everywhere From websites and apps that attract millions of users, through to communities and services that engage local people. They may from time to time provide feedback or suggestions on how you can improve your data. We'll publicise your data to make sure that as many people use it as possible. [See Examples]( {{ site.baseurl }}{% link developer.md %}){:class="button-secondary--ghost" }
[Get Started]( {{ site.baseurl }}{% link getting-started.md %}){: .button-primary}

Use Data

#### Use Data
![what_open_data.png]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/icons/dash-map.svg)
##### Understand what open data is available and how you can help Browse our status dashboard and map to understand the kinds of data available, and read our advocacy guidance to find out how to create more open data. [Status Dashboard]( {{ site.baseurl }}{% link developer.md %}){:class="button-secondary--ghost" } [Open data Map]( {{ site.baseurl }}{% link developer.md %}){:class="button-secondary--ghost" } [Advocacy Guidance]( {{ site.baseurl }}{% link developer.md %}){:class="button-secondary--ghost" }
![what_open_data.png]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/icons/devices.svg)
##### Decide where you want to put the data From websites and apps that attract millions of users, through to communities and services that engage local people, there are lots of ways of using OpenActive data. If you already have a website that engages an audience or an app or a tool, it might make sense to include the data there. It might also make sense for you to build a whole new app, website or service, depending on whether you're a long-term business or a short-term campaign. [See examples of where data has been used]( {{ site.baseurl }}{% link developer.md %}){:class="button-secondary--ghost" }
![what_open_data.png]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/icons/decide.svg)
##### Decide how you want to consume the open data There are many white-label widgets available from organisations who make it easy for you to feature open data on any website. There are also aggregators that provide one API to access all the data, and of course you can collect and display the data directly yourself. [See procurement Guide]( {{ site.baseurl }}{% link developer.md %}){:class="button-secondary--ghost" } [Visit active//choice]( {{ site.baseurl }}{% link developer.md %}){:class="button-secondary--ghost" } [Developer Area]( {{ site.baseurl }}{% link developer.md %}){:class="button-secondary--ghost" }
![what_open_data.png]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/icons/comment.svg)
##### Engage with activity providers and the community Provide feedback on the data via GitHub or reach out directly to the activity provider or booking system. Join the OpenActive community and help use your local or commercial influence to get more data [Feedback guidance]( {{ site.baseurl }}{% link developer.md %}){:class="button-secondary--ghost" } [Join the community]( {{ site.baseurl }}{% link developer.md %}){:class="button-secondary--ghost" }
